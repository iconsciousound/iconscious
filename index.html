Upcoming Shows

Original Roots | Rock | R&B | Reggae
Hailing from New Mexico
I CONSCIOUS is more than just a band—they’re a sonic force, blending the timeless energy of roots, rock, reggae, and R&B. Their live shows take audiences on a powerful journey, fusing classic Jamaican vibes with fresh, island-inspired rhythms that ignite every stage. Their debut EP, featuring the anthem 'Champion,' earned a nomination for Best World Music Recording at the Native American Music Awards and recognition at the Indigenous Peoples Awards in Canada. I CONSCIOUS has had the honor of sharing the stage and learning from Grammy-winning legends, gaining invaluable experience from their performances with international icons. Their latest album, 'Feeling Irie,' is now streaming worldwide, bringing their unique sound to listeners everywhere.
